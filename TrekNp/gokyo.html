Gokyo Lake

Gokyo Lakes and Gokyo Ri Trek:beautiful journey to the enticing Gokyo lakes and Gokyo Ri, which is one of the most amazing viewpoints for great views of Mt. Everest and other Himalayan peaks.

Gokyo Lake

Gokyo Lakes are oligotrophic lakes in Nepal's Sagarmatha National Park, located at an altitude of 4,700–5,000 m (15,400–16,400 ft) above sea level. Lakes and resorts were named after Gokyo Ri peak. These lakes are the world's highest freshwater lake system comprising six main lakes, of which Thonak Lake is the largest.In September 2007, Gokyo and its associated wetlands of 7,770 ha (30.0 sq mi) have been designated a Ramsar site.Gokyo Lakes are located in the Khumjung Village Development Committee of Solukhumbu District in the Sagarmatha Zone in north-eastern Nepal.

Gokyo Lake Guide

  • Kathmandu to Lukla (2840 m) and trek to Phakding (2610 m)
    You will board a public bus or can also take a flight to Lukla if you take road way which is 367km it will take 22-24 hours to reach there so its better to take flight to reach there as it only 30 minutes journey.After reaching to Lukla our trekking journy will start to Phakding which will take 3 hours there and we will be staying for the night there.

  • Phakding to Namche Bazzar (3440 m)
    After reaching Phakding, you will be guided to Namche Bazzar, a beautiful village on the border of Namche and Bhutan. The village has a beautiful waterfall and a stunning view of the Himalayan range. After visiting Namche Bazzar, you will continue to Namche, the capital of Namche Bhutan, and then to Ghats, a beautiful city with a beautiful river flowing through it.

  • Namche Bazzar to Dole (4200 m)
     Early morning we head towards Dole, a hike that takes around 5 hours of walking. First of all our path moves downhill, then we climb uphill and pass the Mon La pass before reaching Phortse Tenga. On the way, the rhododendron forest, birch forest covered with hanging lichens and mosses will keep you energized all the time. We then ascend towards our final destination for today,Dole, where we spend our night at Teahouse.

  • Dole to Macchherma (4470 m)
    Machherna, situated at the height of 4470 m, is our destination for today. We hike about 4 hours uphill. Significantly, the marvelous vistas of Cho Oyu, Kantega, and Thamserku will mesmerize you. Upon arrival in Machherma, we end our day at a Teahouse.

  • Macchherma to Gokyo Ri (4790 m)
    Today is one of the most awaited days of the whole trek because we ascend towards Gokyo Ri, lying at the elevation of 4750 m, hiking approximately 5 hours. We pass Pangka and later walk on the messy, steep moraines of Ngozumpa Glacier – the major glacier in Nepal. We then get a stunning view of greenish-blue Gokyo Lake. The varied scenery of the Gokyo Valley, adjacent peaks flanking Tibet, and Cho Oyu will, for sure, take away your breath. We then head towards the beautiful second lake of stunning Gokyo Lake. At the shore lying on the east, we can notice the settlement of Gokyo. Overnight at Teahouse.

  • Hike from Gokyo Ri to Gokyo Peak (5360 m)
    On this day we hike up to Gokyo Peak for around 3 hours and reach an elevation of 5360 m. From the peak, we witness the stunning panorama of Mt. Everest, Makalu, etc. After enjoying the stunning landscapes for a while, we ascend back again till we reach Dole. Overnight at Teahouse.

  • Trek down to Khumjung Village (3780 m)
     Today we trek to Khumjung village located at an elevation of 3780 m. Walking further, we reach the hidden valley of Khumjung village, one of the most important villages of the Everest Region. Here we make a trip to Khumjung monastery, where we also get a sight of the nearby Sherpa village. Overnight at Teahouse.

  • Trek down to Manjo (2835 m)
     We leave Khumjung village to head towards Syangboche, where we get to view the stunning peaks. We then walk to Thame village, where we rest for a while. As we depart from Thami, we walk across Bhote Khola on a bridge. We will after that walk all the way from side to side with the area of Thamo and get there at Namche Bazaar. Walking down, we will cross the large suspension bridge above the Dudh Kosi River and then pass all the way through Jorsale to get to Manjo. Overnight at Teahouse.

  • Trek back to Lukla (2840 m)
     This is the last day of the trek. We will walk ahead from Manjo to get to Lukla which is situated at the altitude of 2840 m taking approximately 5 hours. Here we will celebrate the success of our trek. Overnight at Teahouse.

  • Early morning flight to Kathmandu (1400 m)
    Early in the morning, we will fly to Kathmandu on this day. After that, we will transfer to our hotel in Kathmandu. Upon arrival in Kathmandu, we do some leisure activities and Sightseeing in the Thamel area, a famous tourist area in Kathmandu. In the evening, time for leisure and shopping.
