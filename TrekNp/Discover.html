Unveil Nepal's Mystical Treasures

Let's Embark on a journey to uncover Nepal's beauty and culture.


Everest Base Camp

Majestic Mt. Everest: the world's highest peak, has awe-inspiring beauty, drawing adventurers and offering breathtaking vistas in its rugged majesty.


Panch Pokhari

Panch Pokhari: offers stunning alpine lakes, pristine wilderness, panoramic mountain views, cultural encounters, and rewarding trekking experiences.


Tilicho Lake

Tilicho Lake Trek: turquoise gem amidst snowy peaks, challenging trails, serene landscapes, cultural encounters, and unforgettable Himalayan adventure.


Langtang Valley Trek

Langtang Valley Trek: showcases majestic mountains, verdant forests, glacial valleys, Tamang, diverse wildlife, and a serene trekking experience in a pristine natural setting.


Shey Phoksundo Lake

Shey Phoksundo Lake: showcases turquoise waters, rugged landscapes, ancient monasteries, nomadic cultures, and a remote Himalayan wilderness experience.


Khaptad National Park

Khaptad National Park: offers lush forests, diverse wildlife, alpine meadows, cultural heritage, spiritual sites, and serene Himalayan landscapes.


Mardi Himal

The Mardi Himal Trek:offers its own unique experience. The beauty of this journey reflects from the beautiful natural gifts and the cultural beauty of the remote settlements.


Rara Lake

Rara Lake: captivates with its turquoise waters, surrounding forests, diverse wildlife, remote tranquility, and serene beauty in the Himalayas.


Kuri Village

Kuri village:lies at an altitude of 3,842 meters above the sea level This village is famous for its beautiful landscape and splendid panoramic view of the Himalayan range.


Khumai Dada

Khumai Dada: features panoramic views, terraced fields, traditional villages, cultural experiences, and a tranquil journey through rural landscapes.


Manaslu Trek

Manaslu Circuit Trek:one of those classical treks where you get to experience the grandeur of spectacular Himalayan ranges and appealing natural beauty amid remoteness.


Upper Mustang

Trekking in Upper Mustang:exploration of thousand-year-old monasteries, caves, canyons, desert beauty, cliff-faced landscapes, local tribes, and their incredible culture.


Poon Hill

The Ghorepani Poon Hill Trek:offers captivating views of the Annapurna and Dhaulagiri mountain ranges, including stunning sunrise vistas from Poon Hill.


Gosaikunda Trek

Gosaikunda Trek:Popular 4, 5 days moderate trekking in Langtang Region. The trek’s major highlight is the holy Lake of Gosaikunda.


Dolpa Trek

Dopla Trek:isolated deserted wilderness trek is situated in rain shadow of Dhaulagiri, Chureni himal, Gurja himal and many more mountains.


Annapurna Base Camp

Annapurna Base Camp: showcases stunning Himalayan views, diverse landscapes, traditional villages, cultural encounters, and rewarding trekking experiences.


Kanchenjunga Base Camp Trek

The Kanchenjunga Base Camp Trek:awe-inspiring Himalayan journey that leads adventurers to the base of the world's third-highest peak, Kanchenjunga.


Gokyo Lake

Gokyo Lakes and Gokyo Ri Trek:beautiful journey to the enticing Gokyo lakes and Gokyo Ri, which is one of the most amazing viewpoints for great views of Mt. Everest and other Himalayan peaks.
