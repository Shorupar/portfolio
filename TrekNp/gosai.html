Gosaikunda Trek

Gosaikunda Trek:Popular 4, 5 days moderate trekking in Langtang Region. The trek’s major highlight is the holy Lake of Gosaikunda.

Gosaikunda Trek

Gosaikunda is a famous name for the purest holy lake. It is situated in Rasuwa district of Nepal covering a surface with about 13.8 ha (34 acres). It lies, north from Kathmandu between Helambu, Langtang Valley, and Langtang National Park.Gosaikunda Lake (4380m) has great religious significance which is well-known as a pilgrimage site for Hindus who flock to the site in August during Janai Purnima.

Gosaikunda Lake Guide

  • Kathmandu to Dunche (2030 m)
    You will board a public bus to Dunche its 6-7 hours of ride there and we will be staying for the night there.

  • Trek to Dhunche (2030 m)
    After reaching Dunche,next day we will trek to a beautifulplace call Chandan Bari.Its a 5-6 hours of trek and we will be staying there for the night.

  • Trek to Gosaikunda (4380 m)
     Early morning we head towards Gosaikunda our main and prime location of this trek, a hike that takes around 7-8 hours.

  • Trek down to Dhunche (2100 m)
    Next morning we will head bown to Dhunche after a mesmering view og the lake and we will end our day there enjoying the local market and foods.

  • Drive back to Kathmandu
    Early in the morning, we will travel to Kathmandu on this day. After that, we will transfer to our hotel in Kathmandu. Upon arrival in Kathmandu, we do some leisure activities and Sightseeing in the Thamel area, a famous tourist area in Kathmandu. In the evening, time for leisure and shopping.
