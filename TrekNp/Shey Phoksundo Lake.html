Shey Phoksundo Lake

Shey Phoksundo Lake: showcases turquoise waters, rugged landscapes, ancient monasteries, nomadic cultures, and a remote Himalayan wilderness experience.

Shey Phoksundo Lake

Nepal’s deepest and second largest lake, Phoksundo Lake, lies in upper regions of Suligad. Near the lake’s outlet is the country’s highest waterfall. The major rivers in the park are the Khung, Nmajung and Panjang, The Suligad and Jugdual Rivers, which are the major tributaries of the Thuli Bheri River; and the Langu Rilver, which drains the high dolpo plateau to the east and flows westward. Phoksundo Lake is famous for its magnificent turquoise color and te spectacular scenery clearly rank it with the most scenic mountain parks in the world. Ringmo village, a typical Tibetan village, is scenically nestled in the area. Many beautiful glaciers can be found near and above the lake area. The Park contain many Gompas (Monasteries) and religious area. The most famous, Shey Gompa was established in the 11th Century. Thashung Gompa located near Phoksundo Lake was built about 900 years ago to conserve wildlife.
Shey Phoksundo trekking trail ascends and descends through a densely forested path filled with oaks, pines, and fir trees with a view of several waterfalls along the way. Trekking to Phoksundo Lake offers stunning mountain views of snow-capped mountains like Kanjiroba and an exciting walk along the Thulo Bheri and Suligad rivers. It is a short and relatively easy trip to one of Nepal's largest and most spectacular lakes.

Shey Phoksundo Lake Trek Guide

  • Fly from Kathmandu to Nepalgunj (150m/ 492ft)
    You will catch the scenic flight to Nepalgunj from Kathmandu. It is located in the western Terai region, near the Indian Border and Bardia National Park. It is also a commercial and central transportation hub of the country. Later you can visit the Bageshwori temple, one of the oldest temples in Banke District. Explore nearby places; being a small city, you can see a lot around the area and taste the local cuisines. You will embrace cultural diversity from different ethnicities living together within mixed communities.

  • Fly to Juphal (2,502m/ 8,210ft) and Trek to Dunai (2,144m/ 7,035ft)
    After breakfast, you will drive to the airstrip of Nepalgunj to catch the early morning flight to Juphal. Upon landing at Juphal airstrip, you will start your trek following the trail to Dunai. Gradually the path diverse through green meadows and gently trek down to Kalighat. Continue your walk along the riverside of Thulo Bheri, pass Roop Ghar with the watermill, and finally arrive at Dunai. It is the biggest town in the Dolpo district and serves as district headquarters. It lies around the bank of the Thulo Bheri River and is surrounded by green hills. You can visit the local bazaar at Dunai and get familiar with locals, culture, and lifestyles.

  • Trek from Dunai to Chhepka Village (2,670m/ 8,760ft)
    You will start your trek through the forest trail with the pristine views of nature along the banks of the Bheri River. You will cross the river and head north of the river and reach Sulighat, a tiny beautiful village. After walking for a while, you will get the check post of Shey Pohoksundo National Park. It is one of the largest and only trans-Himalayan national parks in Nepal. The park consists of an abundance of magnificent sites that enchants the soul. Next, you will follow the forest trail and continue a downhill walk to a small village of Cheepka.

  • Trek from Chhepka Village to Jharana Village (3,370m/ 11,056ft)
    After leaving Chhepka village, you will head toward one of the best natural waterfall (Jharana) located in the Dolpo region. Walking along the cedar forest trail, you will enjoy the enchanting view of the Bheri valley. You will continue your uphill climb and enjoy the mesmerizing view of Phoksundo Lake's gorge. Enjoying the spectacular view of Jharana, one of the highest waterfalls in Nepal, you will continue your walk to Jharana village.

  • Trek from Jharana to the Tibetan Village of Ringmo (3,459m/ 11,350ft)
    From Jharana waterfall, you will move towards the destination of your trek, Shey Phoksundo Lake. At first, you will make your way to the Tibetan village of Ringmo through the steep downhill path. Passing through the dense forest of birch and coniferous, you will reach Ringmo village. In this Tibetan village, locals' homes and lifestyles are retained securely in the rural mountain hillsides away from the impacts of modernization, a unique site to experience.

  • Trek from Ringmo to Chhepka Village (2,670m/ 8,760ft)
    You will leave Ringmo village with a memorable experience full of thrill and amazement that will stay with you for a lifetime. After having a good breakfast, you will hit the trail again, walk alongside the Phoksundo river. The trail is steep, and you will gradually descend through the beautiful green meadows and encompasses the Renji village. Along the route, the majestic snow-capped mountains will accompany you. Passing through the fir and larch forest, you will trek along the Sulighat river until you reach Chhepka village.

  • Trek from Chhepka to Juphal (2,502m/ 8,210ft)
    Finally, the trek comes to an end with a bunch of memories. After having breakfast at Chhepka, you will continue the downhill excursion on the same forest trail to Juphal. After passing through the beautiful meadows, terraces, fields, and villages, you will reach Juphal. The enchanting vista of the mountains and vibrant meadows will make for an unforgettable trek.

  • Fly from Juphal to Nepalgunj and then to Kathmandu
    Early morning, first you will catch the flight from Juphal to Nepalgunj and then finally to Kathmandu. Both flights offer some fantastic views of the snow-capped peaks, green hills, and beautiful valleys. Upon arrival in Kathmandu, a representative from Third Rock Adventures will escort you to your hotel. The rest of the time, you can enjoy leisure time or stroll around the Thamel area for some last-minute shopping.

  • If everything is planned out, you will return to Kathmandu via flight from Lukla on your nine day.
