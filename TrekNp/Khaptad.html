Khaptad National Park

Khaptad National Park: offers lush forests, diverse wildlife, alpine meadows, cultural heritage, spiritual sites, and serene Himalayan landscapes.

Khaptad National Park Trek

Khaptad National Park has a huge variety of flora. It is like a green carpet rolling down from steep slopes to moorland. The view and the feel of being there is absolutely incomparable and will take travellers to a different place. It supports around 67 species of flora, which harmoniously coexist here. Few of the flora includes rhododendron forests, oak and pine forests. The Khaptad national park map display a great variety of flora also. It has 23 species of mammals and 287 types of birds. This national park is great seeing mammals like leopard, Himalayan black bear, wild dog and musk deer. As it supports a huge number of birds, travellers can see birds like Impeyan pheasant (danphe; Nepalese national bird), Peregrine falcon, the white rumped vulture and many types of partridges, flycatchers, bulbuls, cuckoos and eagles.

Khaptad National Park Trek Guide

  • Fly from Kathmandu to Dhangadi (210 m/690 ft)
    You start your great tour of Khaptad National Park with an hour flight to Dhangadi – a buzzing town in the south-western Nepal. After breakfast, you take a drive to Doti in which you stay overnight in the farmhouse or in the tented camp in Mudegaun village(943m/3094ft) of Doti.

  • Drive to Baghlekh and Trek to Bichpani (3,020 m/9908ft)
    After breakfast you are now ready to trek for the day. At first you drive to Baghlekh passing Silgadhi for about one hour. Start your walk from here. First section before lunch is of two hours walk to Jhigrana (2,300m/7545ft). Lunch at Jhigrana and continue trek to Bichpani (3,020m/9908ft) which takes about four hours. The trek route is beautiful in the dense forest that is rich in medicinal plants. Overnight in the tented camp or basic tea house.

  • Trek to Khaptad (3,010 m/ 9875ft)
    Enjoy your breakfast in the camp and trek to Bukipani for about three hours where you will be served packed lunch carried by our staff. Trek another two hours to Khaptad.

  • Full day Excursion in Khaptad
    Explore Khaptad National Park. We take you to the View tower, Khaptad Baba Ashram, Khaptad Lak, Nagdhunga etc.

  • Trek back to Jhigrana(2250m/7281ft)
    Trek back to Jhigrana. You walk to Bichpani. Enjoy the lunch here and walk another four hours to Jhigrana.

  • Trek back to Baghlekh and Drive to Dhangadi
    Trek back to Baghlekh and from here drive back to Dhangadi. It’s a long day of driving in the mountain region of Nepal, yet your arrival in Dhangadi pays off with buzzing city life after days in the remote mountains. Overnight in the Hotel.

  • Fly to Kathmandu
    Fly from Dhangadi to Kathmandu. In Kathmandu, we transfer you to your hotel and you have leisure time in the evening. Enjoy the city life, enjoy the meal in the restaurant and enjoy rest of the day in leisure. Overnight at the hotel.

  • If everything is planned out, you will return to Kathmandu in seven day.
